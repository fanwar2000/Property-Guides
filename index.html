Smoke Alarm Location
📍
Location
Hallway outside each bedroom (2nd floor), kitchen ceiling near the stove, and basement near the utility room.
  • 1Test smoke detectors at least once a month using the test button.
  • 2Replace batteries regularly or when the detector chirps. Change your clocks, change your batteries!
  • 3Vacuum or dust detectors regularly and follow manufacturer instructions.
  • 4Never borrow a battery from a smoke detector.
  • 5Make sure everyone in the home can hear and recognize the alarm sound.
